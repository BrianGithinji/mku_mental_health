🧠 Understanding Mental Health

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act as we cope with life.

⚠️ Warning Signs to Watch For

  • Persistent sadness or anxiety
  • Difficulty concentrating on studies
  • Changes in sleep or appetite
  • Withdrawal from friends and activities
  • Feeling overwhelmed by daily tasks

💡 Coping Strategies

Study Balance

Take regular breaks, set realistic goals, and maintain a study schedule

Stay Connected

Talk to friends, family, or counselors when feeling stressed

Self-Care

Exercise regularly, eat well, and get adequate sleep

Mindfulness

Practice deep breathing, meditation, or relaxation techniques
